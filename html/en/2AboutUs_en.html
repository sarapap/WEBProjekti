About Us

We are two passionate bakers and food lovers who joined forces to create a place where everyone can enjoy delicious flavor experiences and a warm atmosphere. Our cafe specializes in making cakes and other pastries, not to forget the delights of hot food - here, every taste will surely find something delightful.

Selection

We offer a wide range of delicious cakes and pastries that are full of flavor and love. Choose your favorite drink from our extensive selection of beverages and enjoy fresh coffee or other refreshing drinks.

Specialties

Our specialties include creating custom cakes - we bring your dream cake to life, whether it's a birthday, wedding, or any celebration.

Services

We offer a variety of services, such as space rental for private events, home delivery service, and the option to pick up treats to go.
